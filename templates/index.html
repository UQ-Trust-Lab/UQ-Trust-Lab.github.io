About us

Trust Lab is a dynamic and rapidly growing cybersecurity research team based at the University of Queensland, Australia. Our team, led by A/Prof Guangdong Bai, is composed of passionate cybersecurity enthusiasts. We have established a wide network of collaborations with partner universities in Australia and research institutes globally, allowing us to draw on a diverse range of perspectives and expertise. Our research has been widely recognized, with publications in top-tier conferences and journals.
